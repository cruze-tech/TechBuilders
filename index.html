How to Play

Objective

Design a solar-powered water pumping system for rural communities. Your pump must be powered through wires from a generation source.

Budget and Resources

  • Start with 500 credits to spend on components.
  • Each component has a cost shown in the component library.
  • Your best validated score is saved as your mastery score.

Components

  • Solar Panel: Generates 100W (80 credits)
  • Motor: Consumes 50W (60 credits)
  • Water Pump: Consumes 80W (100 credits)
  • Battery: Supplies 60W (70 credits)
  • Gear: Mechanical support component (30 credits)
  • Wire: Forms electrical connections (10 credits)

Controls

  • Click Component: Place on canvas
  • Drag Component: Move on canvas
  • R: Rotate selected component
  • Delete: Remove selected component

Success Criteria

  • System has positive usable net energy.
  • At least one solar panel is present.
  • The water pump is powered through the wire network.
  • Design remains efficient (2 to 8 components).
