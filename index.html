📖 How to Play
🎯 Objective

Design a solar-powered water pumping system for rural communities. Connect solar panels to provide power for the water pump and complete all objectives to maximize your score.

💰 Budget & Resources
  • Start with 500 credits to spend on components
  • Each component has a cost displayed in the left sidebar
  • Earn points by passing simulations to unlock more budget
⚙️ Components
  • Solar Panel: Generates 100W (costs 80 credits)
  • Motor: Consumes 50W (costs 60 credits)
  • Water Pump: Consumes 80W (costs 100 credits)
  • Battery: Stores 60W energy (costs 70 credits)
  • Gear: Mechanical component (costs 30 credits)
  • Wire: Connects components (costs 10 credits)
🎮 Controls
  • Click: Place components from the library onto the canvas
  • Drag: Move components around the workspace
  • R Key: Rotate selected component
  • Delete Key: Remove selected component
✅ Success Criteria
  • Generate positive net energy (total energy ≥ 0)
  • Use at least one solar panel
  • Include a water pump
  • Keep design efficient (2-6 components)
🧪 Testing Your Design

Click the "Run Test" button to simulate your system. The system will evaluate your design based on the objectives. Score 70+ to complete the challenge!
