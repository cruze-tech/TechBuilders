          How It Works

          • 1. The Mission: You are an engineer solving problems across energy, water, health, transport, and resilience. Read each mission briefing carefully.
          • 2. Build Your System: Pick components from the left panel and place them on the canvas. Balance your budget while adding generators (+W) and loads (−W).
          • 3. Connect with Wires: Place a Wire Link to overlap components. Glowing green means it's powered! No wire = no power.
          • 4. Objectives & Simulation: The right panel lists your goals. Hit Run Simulation to test your build against scenario conditions like weather and demand spikes.
